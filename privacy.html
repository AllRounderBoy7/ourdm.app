2. Data we process

+
    +
  • Phone number for authentication (Firebase Auth).
    • +
  • Optional contacts (only with your consent) to suggest friends; stored locally and not uploaded without consent.
    • +
  • Media/mic/camera access for messages/calls; media is end‑to‑end encrypted in transit.

  • Optional Google Drive backup (appDataFolder scope only) when you connect Drive.

3. Storage & retention

+
    +
  • 1:1 messages are end‑to‑end encrypted. Delivered messages are removed from cloud queues; undelivered copies auto‑delete after 7 days.
    • +
  • “Pocket” and locked chats are stored only on your device, never on Firebase.
    • +
  • Backups live in your Google Drive appDataFolder; you control retention and deletion there.
    • +

4. Sharing

+

We do not sell personal data. Data is only shared with infrastructure providers required to operate the service (Firebase/Google Cloud). No iCloud usage.

+ +

5. Your rights

    +
  • Access/export: You can export chats and account data from Settings.
    • +
  • Deletion: Request account/data deletion via support@ourdm.app; we will process under applicable law.
    • +
  • Consent withdrawal: Disable contact sync or Drive backup in Settings at any time.
