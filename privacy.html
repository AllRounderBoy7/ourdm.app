"At OurDM, owned and operated by Sameer Shah, your privacy is our core mission. We have engineered this platform to ensure that your data remains yours alone."

1
Architecture

Zero-Persistence Design

OurDM operates on a "Transient Relay" model — your messages are never stored a moment longer than needed to reach their destination.

⚡
Auto-Delete on Delivery

Once a message — text, image, or video — is delivered to the recipient's device, it is immediately and permanently purged from our Firebase servers.

🚫
No Logs, Ever

We do not keep logs of who you message, when you messaged, or what was said. No metadata. No activity records. No surveillance.

2
Security

End-to-End Encryption

Every interaction on OurDM is protected with military-grade encryption. The decryption keys live only on your device — nowhere else.

🔐
Keys Stay on Your Device

Neither Sameer Shah nor any third party can intercept or read your conversations. Not the government. Not us. Nobody.

🛡️
TLS in Transit

All data in transit is further protected by TLS — a double layer of security between your device and our relay servers.

3
Media & Status

Ephemeral by Design

Your moments are yours to share — and yours to erase. OurDM treats every piece of media with the same uncompromising privacy standard.

👻
Ghost Status Logic

Status updates are automatically deleted after 24 hours or once viewed by the intended audience — whichever comes first.

📦
Local Processing

Profile pictures are compressed to under 50KB locally on your device before any upload — saving your bandwidth and keeping your data private.

4
Contact Data

Hashed, Never Stored

We only access your contacts to help you find friends on OurDM. That is the only purpose — and we enforce it strictly.

🔑
One-Way Hashing

Contact data is hashed using a one-way algorithm before any processing. The original phone numbers are never readable by our systems.

🗑️
Never Stored Permanently

Contact data is never retained on our infrastructure. Once the matching process is complete, all hashed data is immediately and permanently discarded.
