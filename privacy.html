Identity

Secure Verification via Truecaller

We use the Truecaller SDK to ensure that every user on OurDM is a real person. This eliminates bots and keeps our community safe.

🛡️
One-Tap Auth: Your phone number is verified instantly via Truecaller's secure encrypted tunnel.
🔒
Minimal Access: We only receive your verified number and name. We never access your Truecaller contacts or call logs.
Messaging

Ghost Mode: Zero Data Footprint

OurDM is built on a "Transient" architecture. Your messages are not our property; they are yours.

👻
Auto-Purge: Messages are automatically deleted from our Firebase servers the moment they are delivered to the recipient.
🕵️
No Retention: If a message is not delivered within 30 days, it is permanently deleted. No logs, no backups, no exceptions.
Contacts

Privacy-First Discovery

We only use your contacts to find friends who are already on OurDM. We don't store them.

⚡
Local Hashing: Contact numbers are hashed locally on your device before being compared with our database. We never see the raw numbers.
