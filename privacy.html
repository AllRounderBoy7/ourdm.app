Who we are

Data we process

  • Phone number for authentication via Firebase Auth.
  • Optional contacts (with your consent) for friend suggestions — stored locally unless you opt in.
  • Media, microphone, and camera access for messaging and calls.
  • Optional Google Drive backup using the appDataFolder scope only, when you connect it.

Storage & retention

  • 1:1 messages are end‑to‑end encrypted. Delivered messages are cleared from cloud queues; undelivered copies auto-delete after 7 days.
  • "Pocket" / locked items remain on-device only — never uploaded.
  • Backups live in your Google Drive appDataFolder; you control deletion.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. Data is shared only with infrastructure providers (Firebase / Google) required to run the service. No iCloud integration.

Your rights

  • Export your data anytime from Settings.
  • Request deletion via ourdmofficial@gmail.com.
  • Withdraw consent for contacts or Drive access anytime in Settings.
