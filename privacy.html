Data we process

Storage & retention

+
    +
  • 1:1 messages are end‑to‑end encrypted. Delivered messages are cleared from cloud queues; undelivered copies auto‑delete after 7 days.
    • +
  • “Pocket”/locked items stay on-device only.
    • +
  • Backups live in your Google Drive appDataFolder; you control deletion.
    • +

Sharing

No selling of personal data. Shared only with infrastructure providers (Firebase/Google) to run the service. No iCloud.
